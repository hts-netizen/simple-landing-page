About Kate

Kate Lyster is a designer and entrepreneur from Sandpoint, Idaho. She grew up immersed in the region's outdoor life—skiing, hiking, camping, mountain biking, and life around Lake Pend Oreille.

Raised on an off-grid ranch in the Selkirk Mountains and later apprenticed in her aunt's New York design showroom, Kate developed a design sensibility equally suited to urban lofts and remote cabins.

Through more than two decades of work, she has blended natural influence, architectural awareness, and curation into spaces and collections that feel soulful, refined, and enduring.

Design Philosophy

Grounded in authenticity and craftsmanship, Kate's work explores the dialogue between the built environment and nature.

  • Natural materials and understated luxury
  • Thoughtful spatial planning and architectural coordination
  • Collections curated for timeless daily living
